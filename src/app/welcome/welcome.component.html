Why NanoVault?

NanoVault is an open source web wallet which makes it incredibly easy to send and receive Nano from anywhere in the world.
Fully Client-side Signing
All sensitive operations happen in your browser only - your seed and private keys are never sent across your network.
Stateless Wallet
Because no information is ever stored on a server, there is no account to login to or email address required. Create as many wallets as you want and switch between them easily.
Mobile Friendly
Send and receive Nano from any device, any time, anywhere.
And more
Easily send and receive transactions nearly instantly, store names for your accounts in the address book, and more!
